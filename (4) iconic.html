Iconic Scenes

This page contains some of our favorite scenes from the anime which we cannot get enough of. Beware if you haven’t finished the anime since this section contains spoilers!



1.) Bertholdt and Reiner Reveal

After an intense standoff against the titans at Utgard Castle during season 2, members of the Survey Corps were looking to retreat and rebuild at Trost District. Some members of the Survey Corps already suspected that Reiner and Bertholdt were titan-shifters. However, it took the Survey Corps by surprise as Bertholdt and Reiner suddenly confessed that they were the Colossal Titan and the Armored Titan respectively, out of the blue. Reiner and the other warriors suffered long enough, and he knew that it was time to settle everything once and for all at that moment. All the elements from the setting, build-up, background music, cinematography perfectly captured the essence of that scene along with the characters’ sentiments making it one of the most powerful sequences in the anime.

2.) Levi vs Zeke (Shiganshina District)

One of the things that Attack on Titan truly excels at is fight choreography and animation. During the battle at Shiganshina, Captain Levi is ordered to eliminate the Beast Titan. However, for Levi to get close to the Beast Titan, it had to be distracted with killing most of the Survey Corps. Levi becomes enraged since the Beast Titan makes light of the sacrifices of his comrades, giving us one of the most epic, one-sided titan takedowns in the anime.

3.) Eren Consuming the War Hammer Titan

Eren declares war against Marley by interrupting a conference and massacring many of the attendants. A violent battle between the inhabitants of Paradis Island and the Warriors of Marley ensues, wherein Eren and the Survey Corps come out on top. Eren is unable to inherit the War Hammer Titan’s power due to the holder being encased in crystal which he cannot penetrate. He then forces the Jaw Titan to kill the War Hammer Titan so he can inherit its power. This brutal scene reminds the viewers again of how cruel the world Attack on Titan is set in.

4.) Sasha Eating a Potato During Cadet Initiation

This is the first-time viewers have been introduced to Sasha. During the cadet initiation of the Training Corps, Sasha can be seen eating a steamed potato whilst being lectured by the commander. The commander notices her and begins yelling questions at her. Sasha responds using her unusual reasoning which is centered on her gluttony. This is one of the rare light comedic instances throughout the series.
