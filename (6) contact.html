About the Site

Welcome to Fourth Studio. This is a fan site dedicated to all things Attack on Titan and its community.

Why was this site created?

We wanted to create a website to not only develop our website development skills but to also create a platform that hosts all our interests. In this case, it was the anime and manga series, Attack on Titan. We hope that we can connect with fellow fans through this shared interest and get people who have not delved into the series interested in Attack on Titan.
