Characters

This page contains some of the most popular characters as well as information about them.




Name: Levi Ackerman

Sex: Male

Birthday: December 25 (Capricorn)

Age: Early 30s, 30s, Late 30s (Unspecified throughout the story)

Height: 160 cm

Titan Shifter: No

Occupation: Squad Captain of the Survey Corps

A fan-favorite among the community, Levi is the squad captain of the Special Operations Squad under the Survey Corps. He is regarded as humanity’s strongest soldier due to his high combat prowess and expertise in the usage of the omni-directional mobility gear. Levi is definitely one of the most badass characters the fiction has to offer.







Name: Eren Jaeger

Sex: Male

Birthday: March 30 (Aries)

Age: 15, 19 (post time-skip)

Height: 170 cm, 183 cm (post time-skip)

Titan Shifter: Yes (Attack Titan, Founding Titan, War Hammer Titan)

Occupation: Former Soldier of the Survey Corps (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

The main protagonist of Attack on Titan, Eren, was born and raised in the Shiganshina District. He witnessed the death of his mother to a titan during the Shiganshina District’s wall breach which became his driving force to kill all titans. He then joined the Survey Corps to achieve his goal. Eren can be described as earnest, strong-willed, and impulsive. Watching his journey and character development throughout the story is definitely worth it and will not disappoint you.







Name: Mikasa Ackerman

Sex: Female

Birthday: February 10 (Aquarius)

Age: 15, 19, 22

Height: 170 cm, 176 cm

Titan Shifter: No

Occupation: Officer of the Survey Corps (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

Mikasa is one of Eren’s childhood friends. Ever since they were young, Mikasa can be seen following and protecting Eren. She also displays high combat skills because of her Ackerman lineage. She joined the Survey Corps along with Eren and Armin where she graduated top of her class. Mikasa is involved in multiple action-packed scenes which will surely keep you entertained.







Name: Sasha Braus

Sex: Female

Birthday: July 26 (Leo)

Age: Unspecified

Height: 168 cm, 173 cm

Titan Shifter: No

Occupation: Soldier of the Survey Corps (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

Sasha has become loved by the fans thanks to her character being a comedic relief in such an intense show. She is simple-minded, quirky, somewhat timid, and from time to time exhibits unusual behavior. She also has a huge appetite and will go a long way to satiate herself. Despite all that, Sasha actually has a very sharp intuition and has shown good judgment during adversity.







Name: Historia Reiss

Sex: Female

Birthday: January 15 (Leo)

Age: 15, 19, 22

Height: 145 cm

Titan Shifter: No

Occupation: Royal Queen, Former Soldier of the Survey Corps (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

Historia Reiss is currently the queen of Paradis Island. She was the illegitimate child of Rod Reiss, the head of the Reiss royal family. Due to her illegitimacy, she was banished as a child and forced to take on a new identity. She joined the Training Corps as Krista Lenz and eventually became an important member of the Survey Corps.







Name: Annie Leonhart

Sex: Female

Birthday: March 22 (Aries)

Age: 16, 20, 23

Height: 153 cm

Titan Shifter: Yes (Female Titan)

Occupation: Warrior of Marley’s Warrior Unit, Former Soldier of the Military Police (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

The holder of the Female Titan, Annie was one of the warriors sent from Marley who were tasked to infiltrate Paradis Island to retrieve the Founding titan. Annie physically trained from a young age and has developed an exceptional skill in unarmed combat which she displays when she is in training and in Female Titan form.







Name: Hange Zoë

Sex: Ambiguous

Birthday: J September 5 (Virgo)

Age: Unspecified

Height: 170 cm

Titan Shifter: No

Occupation: Commander of the Survey Corps, Former Squad Leader

Hange is a high-ranking official of the Survey Corps whilst also being a titan researcher. Hange's gender is not specified throughout the series and has not been confirmed by the author either. They are particularly fond of titans and can be extremely energetic about it. Despite their quirky nature, Hange can be calm and collected during serious situations and shows good judgment.







Name: Reiner Braun

Sex: Male

Birthday: August 1 (Leo)

Age: 12, 17, 21, 24

Height: 185 cm, 188 cm

Titan Shifter: Yes (Armored Titan)

Occupation: Vice Captain of Marley’s Warrior Unit, Former Soldier of the Survey Corps (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

Reiner is one of the main antagonists of Attack on Titan. He is the inheritor of the Armored Titan and one of the few candidates tasked with infiltrating Paradis Island. What makes Reiner interesting is that his character is not one-dimensional in terms of being an antagonist. His character shows that there is no absolute good nor bad nations but rather that they are all victims of an unjust world.







Name: Jean Kirstein

Sex: Male

Birthday: April 7 (Aries)

Age: Unspecified

Height: 175 cm, 190 cm

Titan Shifter: No

Occupation: Commanding Officer of the Survey Corps (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class

Jean initially joined the Training Corps aiming to secure an easy and safe life through joining the Military Police. However, this changed during his training, influenced by people like Eren. Jean has a blunt personality which has sparked numerous conflicts. He has also shown leadership qualities and has displayed it during certain battles.







Name: Armin Arlert

Sex: Male

Birthday: January 15 (Leo)

Age: 15, 19, 22

Height: 163 cm, 169 cm

Titan Shifter: Yes (Colossal Titan)

Occupation: Commander of the Survey Corps, Former Officer (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

Armin grew up in Shiganshina along with Eren and Mikasa. At a young age, Armin has always been deeply fascinated about what lies beyond the walls. Although he is rather weak, he has become an important member of the Survey Corps due to his high intelligence and genius strategic skill which has saved the Survey Corps multiple times. He also eventually inherits the Colossal Titan after the battle at Shiganshina.







Name: Erwin Smith

Sex: Male

Birthday: October 14 (Libra)

Age: Unspecified

Height: 188 cm

Titan Shifter: No

Occupation: Commander of the Survey Corps

The 13th Commander of the Survey Corps, Erwin Smith, is known to be discerning, intelligent, respected, and a great leader. Erwin was extremely courageous and often did not hesitate to sacrifice his men when needed. Watching Erwin surely keeps a lot of people hyped up due to his inspiring demeanor.







Name: Bertholdt Hoover

Sex: Male

Birthday: December 30 (Capricorn)

Age: 16

Height: 192 cm

Titan Shifter: Yes (Colossal Titan)

Occupation: Warrior of Marley’s Warrior Unit, Former Soldier of the Survey Corps (Graduated from the Training Corps’ 104th class)

Another warrior from Marley, Bertholdt was the inheritor of the Colossal Titan. Bertholdt was known to be quiet and quite weak-willed, often seen following Reiner around. Although all along Bertholdt always acted as Reiner’s voice of reason and protected him from time to time.







Name: Zeke Jaeger

Sex: Male

Birthday: August 1 (Leo)

Age: 25, 29

Height: 183 cm

Titan Shifter: Yes (Beast Titan)

Occupation: Captain of Marley’s Warrior Unit

As you can guess from his surname, Zeke is related to Eren as his older brother. Zeke is the holder of the Beast Titan; he is said to be the strongest warrior of Marley. Additionally, Zeke possesses high intelligence and is capable of not allowing his emotions to affect his judgment during difficult situations.
